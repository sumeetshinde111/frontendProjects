Patents Added For Biologics

AdisInsight drug profiles now contain a new field in the Drug Properties & Chemical Synopsis section with the title Targets. You can also filter by these targets using a new facet on the drug search results page. The new Target field contains both mechanism-based and non-mechanism-based targets. This allows users to see all the targets for a drug in a single place regardless of action. As before, Mechanisms of Action in AdisInsight are still target-based allowing users to u nderstand the action the drug is taking to produce the therapeutic effect.
